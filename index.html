Focus Tube

A few lines of javascript that let me search YouTube's site without all the irresistable video suggestions on their normal home page. The check box searches on a seperate project (not mine) that loops youtube videos with no autoplay or suggested videos sidebar.

For all the times I've tried to look up how peel a hard boiled egg or exit vim and accidentally found myself watching vine compilations or russian traffic fails twenty minutes (or two hours) later.
